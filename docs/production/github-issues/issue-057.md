# Issue 057: Implement store expansion mechanic

**Wave**: wave-3
**Milestone**: M3 Progression + Content Expansion
**Labels**: `gameplay`, `progression`, `phase:m3`, `priority:low`
**Dependencies**: issue-046, issue-047

## Why This Matters

Physical growth makes success tangible.

## Scope

Player can buy adjacent retail space to expand store floor area. More floor space = more shelves = more capacity. Expansion costs money and requires reputation threshold.

## Deliverables

- Expansion option in store management UI
- Cost and reputation requirements
- Store scene expands to show larger area
- More fixture slots available after expansion

## Acceptance Criteria

- Can purchase expansion when requirements met
- Store visibly grows
- More shelves can be placed
- Expansion persists in save
